Panda-X is a Hive Blockchain delegation service designed to provide a stable and rewarding experience. We work to promote a sustainable ecosystem of organic curation and we are committed to key elements like delegation rewards, token buy-back and burn, with compounding funds and strategic investments.

Investment Strategy

Maximize your Hive Power (HP) with Panda-X delegation and dynamic investment strategy. Delegate your Hive Power to @pandex account to directly support content creators and simultaneously earn 70% of the curation rewards distributed as liquid Hive and PANDA Token, 10% of the curation rewards go into strategic investments, potentially increasing your returns over time. Participate in decentralized curation by using both HP and staked PANDA tokens to vote on contents. This comprehensive system fosters continuous growth, distributes dividends, and you as a key contributor to content curation on Hive.
